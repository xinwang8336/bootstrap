Great Online Degrees for Adult Students

Are you an adult who is looking to further your education, but can't commit to a traditional class schedule? Whether you're a working professional, a busy parent, or just have a hectic agenda, an online degree program may be the answer.

In fact, many students are going online to change or further their careers, according to a study entitled "Online College Students 2012" by The Learning House, an organization that helps colleges develop online degree programs. The study revealed that 46 percent of online students had the goal of advancing in their current career field, while 29 percent were looking to change careers.
